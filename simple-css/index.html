Simple CSS

This page shows heading text and paragraph text inside of a div with a blue background, which is inside of a div with a green background, which is inside of a div with a red background, which is inside of a div with no background defined and a width of 400 pixels. All of the divs have a black border 5 pixels thick with 20 pixels of padding on the inside of the border (that's why this text doesn't extend all the way to the border, and why the borders of the divs don't touch each other). The heights of all the divs are determined by the amount of text here in the inner-most div as well as the amount of accumulated padding and border thickness.
